亚、跨、超声速风洞实验平台结束谈判

大连理工大学航空航天学院空气动力学实验室亚跨超声速实验平台结束商业谈判。最终沈阳航空航天大学科技发展中心的方案成功中标。该方案为暂冲式，以两台大型压气机为气源，实验段截面20cmx20cm，最大风速可达2马赫，其他各方面参数符合实验室提出的要求。该中心在高速风洞设计制造上有着丰富的经验，且价格和工期合理，并提供有力的售后保障。平台预计2012年9月建成，建成后将对大连理工大学在高速气动力学研究方面起到极大的推动作用。

A contract of building a supersonic wind tunnel test platform was signed with Shenyang UADC on Nov. 27, 2011 by Dr. Gao representing DUT. This is a blow down type of tunnel using two air compressors each comsumes 150KW of power. Compressed Air with a pressure up to 170 PSI will be stored in a few tanks with a total volume of 200 cubit meters. These allow the maximum velocity in the 20cm by 20cm test section reach a Mach number of 2.0 for a duration of approximately 45 seconds. The facility will be completed by Sept. 2012. The addition of this state-of-art facility will help the researchers in DUT work on solving some of the fundamental issues in compressible flow theories, as well as attract industrial support in research activities. Dr. Gao from Turbulence Control Lab participated the design of this facility.
