高南老师获得学校基本科研业务费资助

Dr. Gao received funding from the University for flapping wing research

实验室高南老师获得大连理工大学2011年基本科研业务费理科专项的支持，金额10万元，时间期限为2011-2012，课题题目为“扑翼飞行器的流场特性”。到目前为止理工大学共为湍流实验室高南老师提供两项共22万元资助，这对实验室的初期建设起到了关键的作用。

The Dalian University of Technology offer a 100,000 RMB Yuan grant to Dr. Gao of Turbulence Control Lab to investigate the flow structures around a flapping wing. This will be helpful in further understanding the flow around a moving surface, which is of importance for a range of industrial applications and in developing better turbulence models. Up to now, the university has already invested a total of 240,000 Yuan in Turbulence Control Lab through two grants to Dr. Gao. The university also invested 500,000 Yuan in setting up a machine shop in the Lab and another 300,000 Yuan for the flow visualization facilities through a grant offered to Prof. Chuijie Wu. The University will offer another 2.7 million Yuan in the year 2012 to build a supersonic wind tunnel in the Turbulence Control Lab.
