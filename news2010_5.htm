李迎军加入研究组

硕士生李迎军同学加入研究组，他2010年毕业于兰州大学力学系。他的研究方向为周期运动的边界和大尺度涡旋结构的相互作用。主要通过实验方法对运动边界影响下的流场进行研究，以确定涡旋发展规律，以及对壁面换热等影响。

Master student Yingjun Li joined the Lab in Sept 2010. Mr. Li graduated from the department of Mechnics of Lanzhou University. His research topic in the Turbulence Control Lab will be the interactions between a compliant surface and the large scale flow structures in a planar jet. He will make detailed measurements of flow, structural vibrations and heat transfer using state-of-art instruments and seek the dynamic relations among various factors.
