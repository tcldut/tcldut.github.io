航空航天学院空气动力学实验室通过验收投入使用 The constructions of Aerodynamics Laboratory were completed

经过近1年时间的建设，航空航天学院实验室在2010年4月底正式通过验收并投入使用。空气动力学实验室建筑面积1000平方米。未来若干年内，航院科研人员将在这个空间内开展新概念飞行器及相关基础科学问题的实验研究。

After 7 months of construction work, the Aerodynamics Laboratory of the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics was completed in the end of April 2010. Four faculty members and their research groups will carry out scientific research and undergraduate laboratories in this facility. One of these groups, Turbulence Control Lab, will be lead by Dr. Gao and will be working on controlling the turbulent flow structures in separated flows for a safer aircraft and more efficient mixing process to save energy.
