°∞English Presentation of Aviation History°± was wrapped up on Dec. 2nd 2010. Twenty four presentations were given in five weeks time. The topics cover a range of prominent aircraft models through aviation history, from the early propeller driven civil airliners to the latest military fighter jets. 24 year 2 Aerospace Engineering students participated in this activity, everyone delivered a 15 minutes presentation about the aircraft they were assigned and there was 5 minutes question time after every presentation. The content of the presentation covered the key features of the aircraft, their designers and the previous and later models, and some interesting stories related to the aircraft. Students collected the materials and prepared the PowerPoint slides independently. This activity was organized by Dr. Gao of Turbulence Control Lab.
