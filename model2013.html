飞机设计及模型制作Aircraft Design and Model Fabrication

2013 Summer(June 24-July7, 2013)

Undergraduate compulsory course for 25 3rd year aerospace engineering students, 2 credits

12-hour lecture plus 32-hour model building, lectured in Chinese

分成5组，每组最多6人，推选组长1名
1天内提出设计目标
2周时间内完成一架模型飞机设计制作
材料：KT板,木板，20A电调，无刷电机
考核方式：设计报告50%, 飞行表演50%

Text:
1.《飞机总体设计》顾诵芬 北航出版社2001
2.Aircraft design: a conceptual approach, D.P. Raymer 1992, AIAA

Readings: 其他读物
Aircraft flight: a description of the physical principles of aircraft flight, 4th edition, Barnard and Philpott, Pearson
